FLOE to Be a Part of the Center on Inclusive Software for Learning

December 11, 2017

We're pleased to announce that tools and knowledge from FLOE work on inclusive open education will be a part of to the newly-announced Center on Inclusive Software for Learning (CISL) as part of the Inclusive Design Research Centre's partnership on the project.

Led by CAST and funded by the the U.S. Department of Education's Office of Special Education Programs, CISL will support the development of an open-source software suite, industry guidelines and suporting research to ensure K-12 students get engaging personalized learning through Open Education Resources.

We look forward to working with CAST and project partner SRI International on advancing the state of the art in inclusive open education with FLOE!

Floe is a project of the Inclusive Design Research Centre, OCAD University.

Funded by a grant from The William and Flora Hewlett Foundation.
